Gem Collector Game

Instructions:

  • There are four gems displayed at the bottom of the page, they each have a random value.
  • Whenever a gem is clicked, it will add a specific amount of points to your score.
  • The objective of the game is to add points in order to equal the computer number.
  • You lose if your points go beyond the computer number.
  • You win if you match the number.

  • Thanks for playing, good luck!
